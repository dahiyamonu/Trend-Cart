about us
As the team leader of Project Trend Cart, I spearheaded the development of a dynamic and visually appealing e-commerce platform using a robust tech stack. Leveraging HTML, CSS, and JavaScript laid the foundation for a seamless and interactive user experience. Integrating Bootstrap ensured responsiveness and a consistent design across devices. The incorporation of React elevated our project with its component-based architecture, enhancing both development efficiency and user engagement. Through effective collaboration and innovative use of technologies, Team Trend Cart delivered a cutting-edge online shopping experience.
